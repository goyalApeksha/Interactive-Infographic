Troubleshooting

Resolve common issues and continue progressing towards your goals.

1. Unable to Access My Account

If you're having trouble logging in, try the following steps:

  • Check your internet connection.
  • Ensure your username and password are entered correctly.
  • If you've forgotten your password, click here to reset it.
  • Clear your browser cache and cookies.
  • Try using a different browser or device.

2. Interactive Infographic Not Loading Properly

If the infographic isn't displaying correctly, follow these steps:

  • Ensure your device is connected to the internet.
  • Refresh the page to reload the content.
  • Update your browser to the latest version.
  • Disable browser extensions or try an incognito window.
  • If the issue persists, contact support at reachzenithheights@gmail.com.

3. Goal Tracking Not Saving

If you're facing issues with saving your goals or progress:

  • Ensure you're logged in to your account.
  • Check your internet connection to ensure it’s stable.
  • Clear your browser’s cache to fix any issues with old data.
  • If the problem persists, try using a different device.
  • Contact us if the issue continues.

4. Inaccurate or Missing Data

If you notice incorrect or missing data in your profile or goals:

  • Check your profile for any incomplete information.
  • Ensure you've submitted all required data for goal tracking.
  • Try logging out and logging back in to sync your data.
  • If the issue isn't resolved, please contact us for assistance.
